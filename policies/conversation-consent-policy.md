# Conversation Consent Policy

Call recordings and live transcripts may contain sensitive personal data.

## Required Before Processing

The user must confirm they have the right to process the transcript or recording.

If not confirmed:

- do not analyze the content
- provide setup guidance only
- ask the user to confirm consent/permission

## Allowed Without Audio

If the user provides only meeting notes or a summary, analyze the text as user-provided context.
Do not claim sentiment, talk/listen ratio, or exact quotes unless the transcript supports it.

## CRM Sync

Call notes, action items, and stage recommendations still require approval before CRM write-back.
