# Privacy

Pipeline-to-Proposal runs entirely inside your Claude environment.

This plugin does not collect, transmit, or store any data about you, your prospects, your CRM, or
your conversations.

All data stays between you and Claude, subject to Anthropic's privacy policy, and any connected
tools or CRM systems you choose to use, subject to their own policies.

The plugin author, Tommy / Pipeline-to-Proposal, does not receive usage data, prospect data, CRM
records, or conversation content from plugin users through the plugin itself.

If you downloaded this plugin from a landing page or form, that download form may have collected
your name, email, company, or related signup details for product updates, support, and waitlist
communication. That signup flow is separate from the plugin runtime.

You can unsubscribe from product emails at any time by replying to an email or using the unsubscribe
link if one is provided.
