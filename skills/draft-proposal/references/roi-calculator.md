# ROI Calculator

Use transparent assumptions. Never invent customer numbers; ask or mark placeholders.

## Time Savings

Formula:

```text
Hours saved per rep per week x number of reps x loaded hourly cost x 52 = annual value
```

Example:

```text
8 hours saved/week x 10 reps x $30/hour x 52 = $124,800/year
```

Use when:

- Sales reps spend time on research, email drafting, proposal writing, CRM notes, or follow-up
  planning.

## Pipeline Acceleration

Formula:

```text
Average deal size x additional won deals per year = revenue impact
```

Safer framing:

```text
If faster preparation and follow-up help the team save even [N] opportunities per year, the revenue
impact is [N] x [average deal size].
```

Use when:

- Slow follow-up or proposal lag is visible.

## New Rep Ramp

Formula:

```text
Months saved to productivity x monthly quota contribution = ramp value
```

Use when:

- Team is hiring SDRs/AEs.
- Sales process is not yet standardized.

## Proposal Quality

Measure with:

- Proposal-to-next-meeting rate.
- Time from discovery call to proposal sent.
- Number of stakeholder-specific proposals created.
- Proposal revision count.

## Vietnamese ROI Framing

```text
Nếu mỗi sales rep tiết kiệm được [X] giờ mỗi tuần cho research, viết email và chuẩn bị proposal,
với team [N] người, phần thời gian thu hồi mỗi năm là:

[X] x [N] x [chi phí/giờ] x 52 = [giá trị/năm]

Mục tiêu không phải thay sales rep, mà là giảm phần việc lặp lại để họ dành nhiều thời gian hơn cho
khách hàng và closing.
```

## Rules

- Use conservative assumptions.
- Show the math.
- Put placeholders when numbers are unknown, using `[NUMBER NEEDS CONFIRMATION]` or
  `[SỐ CẦN XÁC NHẬN]` directly next to the number/formula.
- Do not claim guaranteed revenue lift.
