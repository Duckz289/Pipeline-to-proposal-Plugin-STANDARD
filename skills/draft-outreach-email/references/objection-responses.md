# Objection Responses

Use these to pre-empt or respond to common B2B sales objections.

## "We already use a CRM"

Response angle:

```text
That makes sense. This is not meant to replace the CRM; it sits on top of CRM data and turns it
into research, messaging, proposals, and follow-up recommendations.
```

## "We can do this manually"

Response angle:

```text
Absolutely. The question is whether manual research and drafting is the best use of sales rep time,
especially when the team is scaling or response speed matters.
```

## "AI output is too generic"

Response angle:

```text
Generic output usually happens when AI is not connected to CRM history, internal offer knowledge,
and review rules. The workflow is designed to pull those inputs first and label missing data.
```

## "We are not ready to automate outbound"

Response angle:

```text
The safest first step is not auto-send. It is research, draft generation, and CRM note creation
with human approval.
```

## "Pricing is too high"

Response angle:

```text
The cleanest way to evaluate this is time recovered per rep. If each rep saves even a few hours per
week on research and proposals, the payback can be measured against loaded sales cost.
```

## "We need approval from leadership"

Response angle:

```text
Makes sense. I can send a one-page summary focused on time saved, governance, and rollout risk so
leadership can review quickly.
```

## Vietnamese Variants

### CRM objection

```text
Em hiểu. Bên em không thay CRM, mà dùng dữ liệu CRM để tự động hóa phần research, viết email,
proposal và gợi ý follow-up. CRM vẫn là nguồn dữ liệu chính.
```

### AI generic objection

```text
Đúng, nếu AI không có dữ liệu nội bộ thì output rất dễ chung chung. Điểm khác ở đây là workflow
kéo CRM, knowledge base, pricing/case study rồi mới viết, và phần nào chưa chắc sẽ được ghi rõ.
```

### Not ready to auto-send

```text
Mình không cần auto-send ngay. Giai đoạn đầu có thể chỉ dùng để tạo brief, draft email/proposal và
CRM note, sau đó sales rep duyệt trước khi gửi.
```
